Design Trends 2025

Where nostalgic, handcrafted design meets the precision of new technology. Embracing AI's endless possibilities while craving the quirks and creativity that only come from a human touch.

7 Trends Based on 1B+ designs

Key Insight

"People are embracing the endless possibilities of AI while also craving the quirks, nostalgia, and creativity that only come from a human touch."

The Seven Trends

01
🎨

Analog Meets AI

This trend embodies a blend where timeless techniques like collage and human craftsmanship meet the boundless potential of artificial intelligence. Expect scrapbook aesthetics, handmade textures, and organic imperfections enhanced by AI capabilities.

+92% "scrapbook" searches
02
⬢

Shape Theory

A fresh twist on design that balances nostalgia with innovation. This modular style uses bold geometric shapes to create structure, complemented by back-lit photography and warm color palettes for a personal, inviting touch.

Modular design system Warm color palettes
03
✏️

Refined Grit

Go experimental and edgy with Refined Grit. This trend celebrates handwriting, scribble elements, and raw textures that bring authenticity and edge to designs. It's polished enough to feel professional but raw enough to feel real.

Handwritten typography Raw textures
04
💎

Opulence Era

A return to stripped-back, clean design without the soullessness of monochromatic minimalism. Defined by rich textures, jewel tone palettes, and intricate patterns that bring a sense of luxury and sophistication to your designs.

Jewel tone palettes Rich textures
05
🎪

Serious Fun

Colorful, playful, and nostalgic graphics that don't take themselves too seriously. This trend embraces humor, whimsy, and joy in design while still maintaining professional polish. Perfect for brands wanting to connect on a human level.

+92% "silly" searches +36% "funny" searches +27% "humor" searches
06
🌊

Future in Motion

The trend with the biggest year-over-year growth. From subtle motion gradients to advanced motion typography and animated graphics, movement is becoming essential to modern design. Static is out; dynamic is in.

+109% "motion" searches +78% "animation" searches
07
🌿

Mechanical Botanical

A contrasting blend of soft organic imagery and futuristic design where nature meets technology. This trend combines natural textures and earth tones with sleek digital design elements, creating harmony between the organic and synthetic.

+71% "cutting-edge" searches +32% "cyber" searches
